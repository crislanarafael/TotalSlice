About Me

Hi, my name is Crislana. I want to help you see where your money is going.



Growing up, my mother always hammered in the importance of knowing where your money goes. When I got older, had my first job, paycheck, and credit card, I'll admit and it sounds silly, but I had trouble figuring out where my money went. Bank statements, and bills went over my head. I just couldn't figure out it out!

As a visual learner, numbers often went over my head. So this is why I created Total Slice, a simple tool that SHOWS you where your money is going. Total Slice gives you better context about where you are spending your money with built-in colorful charts, an itemized budget breakdown, and a status bar that updates you on if you are within your monthly budget, or if you need to make some half-time adjustments.

I want to help empower users to make better financial decisions to save for the future. Don't let those bills sneak up on you. You deserve the Total Slice of your money.
